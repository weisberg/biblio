# Biblio Trademark Policy

This policy covers the **Biblio** name, the Biblio logo, and related branding assets.

## Code license vs. trademarks

Biblio's source code is licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 or later (AGPL-3.0-or-later).

That license applies to the code. It does **not** grant any right to use the Biblio name, logo, or branding.

## Allowed without asking

You may, without prior permission:
- refer to Biblio truthfully in commentary, documentation, blog posts, reviews, comparisons, or tutorials
- say that your project is based on, compatible with, or derived from Biblio, as long as that statement is accurate
- redistribute unmodified official releases while preserving existing notices
- make nominative use of the Biblio name to explain that you are discussing the original project

## Not allowed without prior written permission

You may not, without prior written permission:
- use the Biblio name or logo for a modified fork in a way that suggests it is the official project
- rebrand a hosted or commercial offering as "Biblio" if it is not the official Biblio service or product
- use the Biblio logo in product branding, app icons, domains, marketing pages, or store listings for a fork or derivative product
- imply endorsement, affiliation, sponsorship, or approval by the Biblio project, Luca Rossi, or Refactoring when none exists

## Forks and modified versions

If you fork or modify Biblio, you are welcome to do so under the AGPL, but you must choose your own name and branding for any redistributed or hosted version unless you have written permission to use the Biblio marks.

Good examples:
- "Fork of Biblio"
- "Based on Biblio"
- "Internal knowledge app derived from Biblio"

Bad examples:
- calling your modified product "Biblio"
- using the Biblio logo for your fork
- presenting your hosted fork as the official Biblio product

## Reservation of rights

All trademark rights in the Biblio name, logo, and branding are reserved.

Questions or permission requests should be directed to the project maintainer.
